Autobiography of Elder Esther Wambui Muiruri

Birth

Elder Esther Wambui Muiruri was born in 1960 to the late Mr. Benson Boro Kimani and Mrs. Martha Muthoni Boro at Giathieko Village, Kiambu Sub-County, Kiambu County.

Education

Her scholastic journey began at Kanjai Primary School in 1968 where she later sat for Her C.P.E. examination in 1975. She then proceeded to Central Girls' School,Mombasa,before joining Muguga High School,where she did her junior exams.

Service

She served faithfully in the parish, participating in women’s guild, community outreach, and other ministries across the presbytery.

Marriage

In 1989, Elder Esther Muiruri was united in holy matrimony with the late Mr. James Ayub Muiruri. Their marriage is a testimony of God’s grace,blessed with three children,four grandchildren,and one great-grandchild.

Church & Leadership

Church

Elder Esther was born and brought up in a Christian faith family and joined P.C.E.A Kanaji Church where she served in many capacities.

Ordination

She was ordained as an Elder in the year 2000 and has served the parish and presbytery faithfully since then.

Leadership

She served as Church Deacon for six years and Women's Guild Treasurer for three years among other leadership roles.
