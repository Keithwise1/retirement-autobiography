Autobiography of Elder Esther Wambui Muiruri

Birth

Elder Esther Wambui Muiruri was born in 1960 to the late Mr. Benson Boro Kimani and Mrs. Martha Muthoni Boro at Giathieko Village, Kiambu Sub-County, Kiambu County.

Education

Her scholastic journey began at Kanaji Primary (C.P.E. 1975). She later attended Central Girls' School, Muguga High School and Thogoto Teachers Training College.

Service

She served faithfully in the parish—women’s guild, community outreach and multiple leadership roles across the presbytery.

Marriage

In 1989 she was united in holy matrimony with the late Mr. James Ayub Muiruri.
